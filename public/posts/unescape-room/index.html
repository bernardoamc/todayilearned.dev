TIL about unescape() room, a game that helps we improve our Cross-Site Scripting (XSS) skills through challenges.

It’s based on real world examples and becomes increasingly more difficult as each level progress. The page links to a video that explains the vulnerability and helps us to start the game. Give it a try!
