jot command

  • bash

TIL about the jot command. It allows us to print sequential or random data.

The basic option has the format jot [numbers] [start_with] 

$ jot 3 10
10
11
12

or if you want to generate random data: jot -r [numbers] [lower_limit] [upper_limit]

$ jot -r 3 1 20
5
12
19

Not all of the flags are covered here, man jot does a good job explaining them.
